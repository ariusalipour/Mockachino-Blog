Practical shooting, also known as action shooting or dynamic shooting, is a shooting discipline that has gained popularity around the world. Practical shooting is a sport that tests a shooter’s ability to engage targets quickly and accurately while under time pressure and in various scenarios. In this article, we will explore why practical shooting is a great shooting discipline and why it’s worth considering as a hobby or sport.

## IT’S EXCITING AND CHALLENGING

Practical shooting is an exciting and challenging sport that can test a shooter’s skills in a variety of ways. Shooters must be able to move quickly, accurately engage targets, and make split-second decisions. The courses of fire are designed to simulate real-world scenarios, such as defending against multiple attackers or engaging targets from behind cover. This makes the sport much more dynamic and exciting than traditional static target shooting.

## IT FOSTERS A COMMUNITY OF LIKE-MINDED INDIVIDUALS

Practical shooting is a sport that fosters a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for shooting and competition. This community is made up of people from all walks of life, from law enforcement and military personnel to everyday citizens who enjoy shooting as a hobby. This community provides a supportive and encouraging environment that can help shooters improve their skills and build lifelong friendships.

## IT TEACHES SAFE FIREARMS HANDLING

Practical shooting is a sport that emphasizes safe firearms handling. Shooters are required to follow strict safety rules and procedures, which helps to promote safe handling practices. This emphasis on safety can help new shooters learn good habits and develop a respect for firearms.

## IT’S INCLUSIVE

Practical shooting is a sport that is open to shooters of all skill levels, ages, genders, and physical abilities. Shooters are classified based on their skill level, which allows them to compete against others with similar experience and ability. This makes the sport accessible and inclusive, which helps to promote diversity and bring new people into the shooting community.

In conclusion, practical shooting is a great shooting discipline that offers a challenging and exciting sport that promotes safe firearms handling and fosters a supportive community. It’s a great way to improve your shooting skills, meet like-minded individuals, and have fun while doing it. Whether you’re a seasoned shooter or just getting started, practical shooting is definitely worth considering as a hobby or sport.
