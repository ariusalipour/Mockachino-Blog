I am an avid handgun shooter who loves the ability to shoot competitions with a handgun. There are tons of handguns out there, such as the Staccato 2011s and the Laugo Arms Alien handguns. But I’m an eccentric and like to think about what I would actually own in 2023 with the current line up.

Practical shooting competitions require a reliable and accurate handgun to succeed. With so many options available, it can be challenging to determine which one to choose. Here are the top five handguns I’d like to try for practical shooting, along with their specifications and why they’re good for IPSC Handgun.

## 5\. CZ Shadow 2

![](/images/SHO-EXP-TOP/image-10.png)

The CZ Shadow 2 is a highly-regarded gun known for its accuracy and smooth trigger pull. It has a 4.89-inch barrel, an aluminium frame, and a 17-round magazine capacity. The ergonomic design makes it comfortable to hold, and the low bore axis allows for excellent recoil control.

## 4\. Sig Sauer X5 Match

![](/images/SHO-EXP-TOP/image-11.png)

The Sig Sauer X5 Match is a competition-ready handgun with a 5-inch barrel, a stainless steel slide, and a lightweight polymer frame. It has a 17-round magazine capacity, adjustable sights, and a match-grade trigger. The X5 Match is known for its accuracy and reliability, making it an excellent choice for practical shooting competitions.

## 3\. Bul Armory SAS II SAW

![](/images/SHO-EXP-TOP/image-12.png)

The Bul Armory SAS II SAW is a competition-ready gun with a 5.4-inch barrel and a 17-round magazine capacity. It has a steel frame and slide, adjustable sights, and a crisp trigger pull. The SAS II SAW is a reliable and accurate choice for IPSC Handgun competitions, with a reputation for excellent performance.

## 2\. Atlas Gunworks Helios

![](/images/SHO-EXP-TOP/image-14.png)

The Atlas Gunworks Helios is a custom handgun designed specifically for competition shooting. It has a 5-inch barrel, a steel frame and slide, and a 19-round magazine capacity. The Helios features a crisp trigger pull and adjustable sights, making it a highly accurate choice for practical shooting.

## 1\. NightHawk Custom BDS9

![](/images/SHO-EXP-TOP/image-15.png)

The NightHawk Custom BDS9 is a high-end handgun designed for competition shooting. It has a 4.25-inch barrel, a steel frame and slide, and a 17-round magazine capacity. The BDS9 features a match-grade trigger, adjustable sights, and an ergonomic design for comfortable handling. This gun is known for its exceptional accuracy, making it a top choice for practical shooting competitions.

In conclusion, each of these handguns has its strengths and weaknesses. However, they are all excellent choices for practical shooting competitions. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned shooter, finding the right handgun is crucial for success. I would like to have shot each one of these at a match. If only I could own them as this would probably be my list of handguns to own in the cabinet if I had access.
