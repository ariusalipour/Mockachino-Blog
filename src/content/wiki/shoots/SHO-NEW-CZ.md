The CZ Scorpion Evo has become a popular firearm among enthusiasts and practical shooting competitors alike, with its sleek design, reliability and ease of use. Now, the firearm is set to be even more accessible to UK shooters, as CZ has announced the CZ Scorpion Evo .22LR is coming to the UK.

The CZ Scorpion Evo .22LR is a semi-automatic rifle that is chambered in the .22 Long Rifle cartridge. It features a lightweight polymer receiver and a 16.2-inch barrel, which is threaded to accept suppressors or muzzle brakes. The rifle also includes a folding stock, adjustable flip-up sights and a Picatinny rail for mounting accessories.

One of the advantages of the CZ Scorpion Evo .22LR is its affordability. The .22LR cartridge is much cheaper than larger calibres, which means shooters can get more trigger time and training for their money. The rifle is also a great option for those who want to shoot at indoor ranges, where larger calibres may not be allowed due to noise and safety concerns.

CZ has designed the rifle to accept many of the same accessories that are used on the 9mm version of the Scorpion Evo, including handguards, grips and magazines. This means shooters can customize their rifle to fit their individual needs and preferences.

The CZ Scorpion Evo .22LR is also a great option for those who are new to shooting or who want to introduce others to the sport. The .22LR cartridge has very little recoil, which makes it easier for beginners to learn and for experienced shooters to practice their fundamentals. Additionally, the rifle’s adjustable sights and light trigger pull make it easy to hit targets accurately.

One potential drawback of the CZ Scorpion Evo .22LR is its limited range. However, for those who are looking for a rifle to shoot at shorter ranges or for training purposes, the CZ Scorpion Evo .22LR is a great option.

In conclusion, the CZ Scorpion Evo .22LR is an exciting addition to the UK firearms market. Its affordability, versatility and ease of use make it a great option for practical shooting competitors, new shooters and those who want to introduce others to the sport. The rifle’s compatibility with a wide range of accessories also makes it a great platform for customization. Overall, the CZ Scorpion Evo .22LR is an excellent option for anyone who wants a reliable and fun rifle to shoot.
