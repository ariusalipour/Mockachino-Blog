The IWA Outdoor Classics is an annual international trade fair for hunting, shooting sports, outdoor equipment, and civilian and official security products. The event is held in Nuremberg, Germany, and is one of the largest and most important trade fairs of its kind in the world.

The IWA Outdoor Classics is organized by the European Association for Hunting and Shooting Sports, and it brings together exhibitors and visitors from all over the world. The event is designed to showcase the latest products and innovations in the hunting, shooting sports, and outdoor equipment industries. It’s an opportunity for industry professionals to network, establish new business relationships, and learn about the latest trends and technologies.

The event is open to trade visitors only and is not open to the public. Exhibitors at the IWA Outdoor Classics include manufacturers of firearms, ammunition, hunting equipment, optics, knives, outdoor clothing and footwear, as well as distributors, retailers, and service providers.

The IWA Outdoor Classics is a four-day event that features product demonstrations, workshops, seminars, and keynote speeches by experts in the industry. The event is a platform for the launch of new products and innovations in the industry, with many exhibitors using the event to showcase their latest developments to potential customers and business partners.

In addition to the exhibition, the IWA Outdoor Classics also features a shooting range where visitors can test and try out various firearms and other equipment. This shooting range is a popular attraction and is a unique opportunity for visitors to experience the latest equipment and technology first-hand.

The IWA Outdoor Classics is a must-attend event for anyone involved in the hunting, shooting sports, and outdoor equipment industries. It provides a unique opportunity to network with other professionals in the industry, learn about the latest trends and technologies, and experience the latest products and equipment first-hand.
