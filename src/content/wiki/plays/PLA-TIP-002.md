If you are an avid fan of Escape from Tarkov, then you have most definitely seen enough of these posts to make you sick to your stomach. I still think it’s a topic that gets addressed for many newcomers to the game. That being said, they all vary slightly, so here are 5 basic skills you need to learn… in my opinion!

1. **Map Knowledge  
**One of the most important skills to have in Escape From Tarkov is map knowledge. Knowing the layout of the different maps in the game is essential to navigate the terrain, locate loot, and avoid danger. Players should spend time exploring each map and familiarizing themselves with the different areas, landmarks, and chokepoints.
1. **Weapon Handling  
**Another crucial skill to master in Escape From Tarkov is weapon handling. Players should understand the mechanics of each weapon they use, including recoil, bullet drop, and bullet velocity. Learning how to aim accurately, manage recoil, and control their weapon in different situations will improve a player’s chances of survival.
1. **Communication**  
Escape From Tarkov is a team-based game, and communication is key to success. Players should learn how to communicate effectively with their team, including callouts for enemy positions, team movements, and resource sharing. Communication can make a difference in intense firefights and prevent friendly fire incidents.
1. **Resource Management  
**Managing resources such as ammunition, medical supplies, and food and drink is essential in Escape From Tarkov. Players should learn how to manage their resources effectively to avoid running out of critical items during a raid. This includes knowing where to find resources on the maps, prioritizing essential items, and conserving resources when possible.
1. **Patience and Persistence  
**Escape From Tarkov is a challenging game that requires patience and persistence. Players should learn to be patient and take their time when exploring the maps, looting, and engaging enemies. They should also be persistent in their efforts to improve their skills, even when faced with setbacks and failures.
In conclusion, Escape From Tarkov is a complex and challenging game that requires players to learn and master different skills to succeed. Map knowledge, weapon handling, communication, resource management, and patience and persistence are some of the essential skills to improve your gameplay. By learning and practicing these skills, players can increase their chances of surviving raids and achieving success in Escape From Tarkov.
